Valuations
Our Group
We are one of the largest, fully integrated real estate valuation organization in Mombasa County and is the foremost valuation advisor to leading institutional investors and lenders in their critical equity and debt investment decisions. We provide appraisal services, highest and best use analysis, and arbitration services and specialized expertise in various industry sectors. With professionals specializing in valuation, we are uniquely qualified to undertake any valuation assignment.
We are a profession firm spanning over fifty years dealing in the valuation of real estates mainly for mortgage purposes where we undertake valuation for institution such as :-
1. Compulsory acquisition valuation.
2. Rental Valuation
3. Plant Machinery and automobile valuation.
4. Furniture, fittings, equipment, trading stock and going concerns.
